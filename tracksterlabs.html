Trackster Labs

Series Brief:- A darkly comedic web series about a few sentient robots that continue to toil in the darker places of an unknown and abandoned lab. They continue to function without ever knowing that the world outside has perished. In their efforts to maintain the lab they encounter the menacing and mysterious CORE systems that overall govern the sytems of the labs and learn of it's secrets.
