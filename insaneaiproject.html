Game Brief:- Sports AR fitness game with a dedicated story.
Genre:- SciFi, Adventure, Mystery, Fitness Game, AR, Hindu Mythology
Employer:- INSANE AI
Employer Feedback:- INSANE AI feedback
Role:- Narrative Designer

Main Responsibilities

  • Pitched, Envisioned and wrote multiple different pilot worlds and story Ideas.
  • Devised narrative methodology and logic for Augmented Reality (AR) gameplay.
  • Conceived and wrote narrative content for most aspects of the game.
  • Created dynamic themes focused on the product and product user.
  • Created the main world, history, an intricate political system and the lore for the game.
  • Wrote compelling characters that suited the given theme and world.
  • Wrote a working screenplay for the entire game in collaboration with the production team.
  • Envisioned and wrote extra content required to create added depth to the game.
  • Maintained & tracked the story and world logic.
  • Coordinated with engineering and design team to design Levels and overall gameplay specifics.
  • Provided written cues for voice acting, cinematic direction and asset generation.
  • Performed Research and investigated topics to aid in adding depth to the world built.

About the work:

  • I was tasked to create multiple pitches for fantastical/intriguing worlds that delved into themes of body and rejuvenation as that was the focus of the product. I learnt in and out about the product, read more within the field and developed the idea of "Death through disuse". This theme revolved around how disuse of anything would lead to its disrepair and then eventual diminishment, this suited the health based theme of the product, so I took it as the main theme of our game. Around this, I created a world of the game.
  • The main synopsis of the story takes place in a long lost underground city, isolated from the rest of the unknown world where people survive on a singular resource known as Amrit. A refined resource that they used to keep themselves alive, there's no food, there's no water, there's no electricity, they can only subside on this feint source of energy they mine in veins they can find nearby their city. This forms the main part of their lives as everything revolves around it. There are intricate class systems, divided among different levels of the city where people perform different actions based on the kind of caste systems they are born in. The city is also an ever eternal abode, no one can truly leave it for a better place even if they wanted to. The city has massive conduits that run these Amrit through them to power the city, but it has a side effect of creating a shimmer like effect outside it called the Chakramaya. This effect makes people delirious, changes how they behave, mutates them and can even perform temporal things that are beyond normal comprehension, so people tend to stay.
  • Our story takes place when the protagonist is forced to enter the shimmer-like chakra maya where he is put within the influence of the temporal warp and is forced to go through a journey of discovery of a conspiracy and self-discovery through the help of an ancient technology.
